    WHAT WE DO

    Restoring ecosystems, empowering communities & Making the World Greener

    Forest Restoration

    We work with communities to restore degraded forests in Kerio Valley and beyond, ensuring biodiversity thrives for generations.

    Read More

    Water Conservation

    Protecting rivers, springs, and catchment areas is at the heart of our mission to safeguard clean water for communities and wildlife.

    Read More

    Sustainable Land Use

    We promote sustainable farming, recycling, and soil management to restore land productivity and improve livelihoods.

    Read More

    Renewable Energy

    Supporting solar and clean energy adoption reduces pressure on forests and contributes to climate resilience.

    Read More

    Community Empowerment

    We strengthen local communities through training, capacity building, and sustainable economic opportunities.

    Read More

    Ecosystem Restoration
